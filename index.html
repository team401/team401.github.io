Who are we?

We are a FIRST Robotics Competition team deicated spreading STEM and serving our community. Team 401 was founded in 1999 by five Virginia Tech students. At our founding, we were known as PowerSurge, and between the years of 2009 and 2016 we held the name HokieGUARD. Now, we are known as the Copperheads. We have moved around to different locations over the years, and have now had a stable shop space in Christiansburg High School for a few years. We have built a strong robotics program along with a constantly expanding outreach program. Click here to learn about our competition history and here to watch us live during competition. From March 24-26, 2023 we will be competing in Glen Allen, VA

What do we do?

Currently, our team is made up of about 30 people from Blacksburg and Christiansburg High Schools. We accept students from all four high schools, but our membership usually stems mostly from these two schools. Each of our students fulfill the many different roles required to run our team, ranging from team leadership, outreach organization, mechanical design, parts manufacturing, and finance organization.
